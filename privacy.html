Privacy Policy

Your privacy and medical data security are our top priorities. Learn how we protect your X-ray images and personal information.

 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information and medical data when you use our X-ray analysis service. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and medical privacy.

 Information We Collect

Medical Information

  • X-ray Images: Digital X-ray images you upload for analysis
  • Analysis Results: Fracture detection results and disease identification
  • Medical Recommendations: Treatment suggestions and next steps provided by our system

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Upload timestamps and session data
  • System performance metrics

Important: We DON'T collect or store personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or medical record numbers unless explicitly provided by you.

 How We Use Your Information

Your medical data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Medical Analysis: Processing X-ray images to detect fractures and diseases
  • Treatment Recommendations: Providing appropriate medical guidance and next steps
  • Service Improvement: Enhancing our AI algorithms and diagnostic accuracy
  • Quality Assurance: Ensuring the reliability and safety of our analysis system

 Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive medical information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Storage: Medical images are stored in HIPAA-compliant, secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access Control: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data
  • Data Anonymization: All uploaded images are automatically anonymized to remove identifying information
  • Automatic Deletion: X-ray images are automatically deleted after 30 days unless you request otherwise

 Data Retention & Deletion

Automatic Deletion

To protect your privacy, we automatically delete your uploaded X-ray images and analysis results after 30 days from upload.

Manual Deletion

You can request immediate deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team. We will process deletion requests within 48 hours.

Research Data

Anonymous, non-identifiable data may be retained for research purposes to improve our diagnostic algorithms. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

 Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your medical data:

  • Access: Request copies of your uploaded images and analysis results
  • Deletion: Request immediate deletion of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Opt-out: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

 Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal medical information to third parties. Limited sharing may occur in these situations:

  • Medical Professionals: With your explicit consent, we may share results with your healthcare provider
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Emergency Situations: To protect your health and safety in critical situations
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

 Medical Disclaimer

Important Medical Disclaimer: Our X-ray analysis service is designed to assist in medical diagnosis but should NOT be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

 Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Session Management: To maintain your upload session

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

 International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries with different privacy laws. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions by regulatory authorities
  • Certified data protection frameworks

 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications (if you've provided your email)
  • Website banner notifications
  • Updated "Last Modified" date below
