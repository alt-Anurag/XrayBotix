  General Information

XrayBotix is an AI-powered medical imaging analysis platform that helps healthcare professionals and patients understand X-ray images. Our advanced machine learning algorithms can detect fractures, analyze bone conditions, and provide detailed insights about various medical conditions visible in X-ray scans.

The system is designed to assist in medical diagnosis by providing fast, accurate, and detailed analysis of X-ray images across multiple body parts including chest, hands, legs, spine, and dental regions.

XrayBotix uses state-of-the-art deep learning models trained on thousands of medical images with an accuracy rate of over 95% for fracture detection and 92% for disease identification.

Important: While our AI provides highly accurate analysis, it should always be used as a supplementary tool alongside professional medical consultation. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for final diagnosis and treatment decisions.

  X-ray Types & Analysis

XrayBotix currently supports analysis for the following X-ray types:

  • Chest X-rays: Lung conditions, pneumonia, fractures, heart abnormalities
  • Hand X-rays: Finger fractures, wrist injuries, arthritis, bone deformities
  • Leg X-rays: Femur, tibia, fibula fractures, knee joint issues
  • Spine X-rays: Vertebral fractures, scoliosis, disc problems
  • Dental X-rays: Tooth decay, root issues, jaw problems, impacted teeth

Each type uses specialized AI models trained specifically for that anatomical region.

XrayBotix supports the following image formats:

  • DICOM files (.dcm) - Medical imaging standard
  • JPEG images (.jpg, .jpeg) - Common format
  • PNG images (.png) - High quality format
  • TIFF images (.tiff, .tif) - Medical grade quality

Maximum file size: 50MB per image. For best results, use high-resolution images with good contrast.

Our AI can detect and classify various types of fractures including:

  • Simple fractures: Clean break in the bone
  • Compound fractures: Bone breaks through the skin
  • Hairline fractures: Small cracks in the bone
  • Comminuted fractures: Bone breaks into multiple pieces
  • Stress fractures: Small cracks from repeated stress
  • Displaced fractures: Bone pieces are misaligned

The system also provides severity assessment and healing recommendations for each detected fracture.

  Analysis & Results

Analysis time depends on the complexity and type of X-ray:

  • Simple fracture detection: 15-30 seconds
  • Comprehensive analysis: 1-2 minutes
  • Complex cases: 2-5 minutes
  • Batch processing: 3-10 minutes per image

Our AI continuously processes in the background and provides real-time progress updates during analysis.

Each analysis report includes:

  • Detected conditions: Fractures, diseases, abnormalities
  • Severity assessment: Mild, moderate, or severe
  • Location mapping: Precise anatomical locations
  • Confidence scores: AI certainty levels
  • Recommended actions: Immediate care, specialist referral
  • Visual annotations: Highlighted areas of concern
  • Comparison data: Normal vs. abnormal findings

If XrayBotix detects a fracture, follow these steps:

  • Immediate care: If severe, seek emergency medical attention
  • Consult a doctor: Schedule appointment with orthopedic specialist
  • Follow recommendations: Review the specific action plan provided
  • Monitor symptoms: Watch for pain, swelling, or mobility issues
  • Download report: Save the analysis for medical consultation

Remember: This AI analysis is a screening tool. Always consult with qualified medical professionals for proper diagnosis and treatment.

  Technical Support

For optimal results, your X-ray image should be:

  • High resolution: At least 1024x1024 pixels
  • Good contrast: Clear differentiation between bone and soft tissue
  • Proper positioning: Anatomical structure clearly visible
  • No artifacts: Minimal noise or distortion

If image quality is poor, our AI will provide quality assessment feedback and suggest improvements for better analysis.
